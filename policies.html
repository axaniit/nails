Booking Gallery Policies

Policies

Please read before booking.

Booking confirmation + deposit

Appointments are not confirmed until I have both approved the request and received the $5 deposit. Submitting a booking request does not automatically reserve your spot.

Pricing

Gel sets are $10 total. Acrylic sets are $15 total. If a design is especially complex or requires me to learn a new technique or buy something extra, there may be an additional $5 charge. The deposit is included in the total price. For example, if you book an acrylic appointment and send the $5 deposit, you would owe $10 at the appointment if there is no added complexity charge.

Late arrivals

If you arrive too late, we may need to reschedule your appointment. If you are less than 20 minutes late, your deposit can be carried over to one rescheduled appointment instead of being lost.

Rescheduling + cancellations

If you need to reschedule and give at least 24 hours notice, your deposit can be applied to your new appointment. Same-day cancellations and last-minute reschedules will result in the deposit being forfeited. Deposits are otherwise nonrefundable.

No-shows

If you miss your appointment without communicating, the deposit will be forfeited. I am still willing to reschedule, but a new deposit may be required.

Location

Enter your preferred location when booking, whether that is my place or somewhere else, and I’ll follow up with you about what works best.

Materials

I have limited materials on hand. If you want something specific, like certain charms, decals, or a very particular color, you are responsible for purchasing it and bringing it to the appointment. Otherwise, you will need to choose from the materials I already have available.

Fills

I offer fills for up to 5 nails or within 2 weeks of your original appointment for 50% of the price you originally paid. Fill appointments do not require a deposit if the remaining amount due for the fill is $5 or less.

Pricing Dislcaimer

Current prices are based on the amount I project I will need to spend restocking, if it turns out that these prices do not allow me to restock without paying out of pocket, prices may need to increase accordingly.

Additional policies may be added later as needed.
